At the Second Meeting of the Parties in 1997, it was decided (following the Report of the Fourth Meeting of the Advisory Committee) that a short report be prepared each year (as required by Article 2(5)) and a more detailed report be submitted every three years covering progress made and the difficulties experienced in implementing the Agreement.  However, at the Fourth Meeting of the Parties in 2003, it was unanimously decided by the Parties that in future only annual reports would be required.

EEA maintains a biodiversity information system at
http://eunis.eea.eu.int/ that provides information on species, habitats
and sites in the context of European legislation.
