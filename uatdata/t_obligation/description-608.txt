EC Water Reporting Obligation. Reporting under Marine Strategy Framework Directive (MSFD) 2008/56/EC for: Art. 8, Art. 9, Art.10; related reporting on geographic areas and regional cooperation (Art. 3/4 & Art.5/6); and metadata regarding data and information used in Art.8 (Art. 19(3)).

Member States shall, in respect of each marine region or subregion concerned, develop and implement a marine strategy for its marine waters,  make an initial assessment, determine a set of characteristics for good environmental status and establish a comprehensive set of environmental targets and associated indicators for their marine waters to guide towards achieving good environmental status in the marine environment taking into account the indicative lists of pressures and impacts and characteristics.
