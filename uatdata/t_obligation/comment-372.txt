Member States have the obligation to: 
Report on programmes related to qualification requirements for personnel involved in recovery, reclamation and destruction; 
Report on defined minimum qualifications and training programmes for personnel responsible for checking leakage in refr. with > 3 kg of ODS; Report on systems established to promote recovery, recycling, reclamation and destruction of ODS. The parameters listed above should only be regarded as indicative. Please refer to the Directive for a full list.
