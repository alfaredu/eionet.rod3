This Regulation (REGULATION (EC) No 1907/2006) replaces Directive 76/769/EEC, Commission Directive 91/155/EEC (1), Commission Directive 93/67/EEC (2), Commission Directive 93/105/EC (3), Commission Directive 2000/21/EC (4), Regulation (EEC) No 793/93 and Commission Regulation (EC) No 1488/94 (5). These Directives and Regulations should therefore be repealed.

This report, together with a summary thereof, shall be forwarded to the Commission in accordance with Article 10 (3) of Regulation (EEC) No 793/93: "The rapporteur for a given priority substance shall evaluate the risk of that substance to man and the environment. Where appropriate, it shall suggest a strategy for limiting these risks, including control measures and/or surveillance programmes."
