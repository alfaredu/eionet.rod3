EU Member States have to report each year by 30 June, following information in electronic format to the Commission, for the previous calendar year: 
quantities of methyl bromide authorised for different treatment for quarantine and pre-shipment purposes used in their territory, specifying the purposes for which methyl bromide was used, and the progress in evaluating and using alternatives;
- quantities of halons installed, used and stored for critical uses, the measures taken to reduce their emissions and an estimate of such emissions, and progress in evaluating and using adequate alternatives;
- cases of illegal trade, in particular those detected during inspections
