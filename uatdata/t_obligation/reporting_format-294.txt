The most recent guidelines were provided by the Commission in Rep. Int. Whal. Commn 48: 292-5.   Explanatory notes are provided in the guidelines for the reports.  General information in the report should cover the intersessional period (or, if a report was not submitted last year, the period since the last report) but statistical information must be provided by calendar year or season.  Papers can be submitted either by e-mail or as good quality single-sided, single-spaced top copies.  If they are not provided by e-mail, Parties should arrange to take an electronic version to the Annual Meeting of the IWC (.pdf format is preferred).
