Member States shall draw up an informative inventory report (IIR) which shall accompany the national emission inventories and projections, in accordance with requirements set out in Table D of Annex I, addressing:

SO2 (sulphur dioxide)
NOx (nitrogen oxides)
NMVOCs (non-methane volatile organic compounds)
NH3 (ammonia)
CO (carbon monoxide)

Particulate matter (PM): particles with an aerodynamic diameter equal to or less than 2.5 micrometres (PM2.5); particles with an aerodynamic diameter equal to or less than 10 micrometres (PM10); black carbon (BC; if available)

Heavy metals (HMs): cadmium (Cd), mercury (Hg), lead (Pb)

Persistent organic pollutants (POPs): total polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs), benzo(a)pyrene (BaP), benzo(b)fluoranthene, benzo(f)fluoranthene, indeno(1,2,3-cd)pyrene, dioxin/furans, polychlorinated biphenyl(PCBs), hexachlorobenzene (HCB)

The following HMs, if available: arsenic (As), chromium (Cr), copper (Cu), nickel (Ni),selenium (Se) and zinc (Zn) and their compounds; total suspended particles (TSP)
