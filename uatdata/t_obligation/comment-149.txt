Member States will submit with results on waste statistics to the Commission a quality report, indicating the degree of precision for the collected data, in accordance with Commission Regulation (EC) No 1445/2005 of 5 September 2005 defining the proper quality evaluation criteria and the contents of the quality reports for waste statistics. A description will be given on the estimations, aggregations or exclusions and the way these procedures affect the distribution of waste categories by economic activities and households.
