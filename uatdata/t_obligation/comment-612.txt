The establishment of marine protected areas including areas already designated or to be designated under the Habitats Directive, the Birds Directive, and under international or regional agreements to which the European Community or Member States concerned are parties.

EEA maintains a biodiversity information system (not compulsory) at http://eunis.eea.europa.eu/ that provides information on species, habitats and sites in the context of European legislation. 

Member States shall take into account relevant measures required under Community legislation, in particular the Water Framework Directive, the UWWT Directive, the New Bathing Water Directive, as well as forthcoming legislation on environmental quality standards in the field of water policy, or international agreements.
