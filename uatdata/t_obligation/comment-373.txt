Member States shall take the necessary arrangements for biocidal products which have been placed on the market to be monitored, to establish whether they comply with the requirements of this Directive, especially as regards effects on water, soil, air and fauna.
Every three years after 14/05/2000, Member States shall forward to the Commission by 30 November of the third year, a report on their action in these matters together with information on any poisonings involving biocidal products. For the first reporting a questionnaire was sent by the Commission to the Member States' designated authorities for guidance purposes. The report for the first period can be found on http://ec.europa.eu/environment/biocides/implementation.htm
