Reporting of information on support provided to developing countries, including information on support for mitigation, adaptation, capacity-building and technology transfer and, if possible, information as to whether financial resources are new and additional.

Information on financial flows based on the so-called "Rio markers" for climate change mitigation-related support and climate change adaptation-related support introduced by the OECD Development Assistance Committee and methodological information concerning the implementation of the climate change Rio markers methodology. Information on private financial flows shall include information on the definitions and methodologies used to determine any figures.
