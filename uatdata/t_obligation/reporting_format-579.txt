Annex III of the Batteries Directive may be adapted or supplemented to take account of technical or scientific progress. In particular detailed rules regarding the calculation of recycling efficiencies shall be added no later than 26 March 2010 and the minimum recycling efficiencies shall be evaluated regularly and adapted to best available techniques. This requirement has been fullfilled by Commission Regulation No 493/2012
