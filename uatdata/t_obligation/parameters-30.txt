Two codelists for groundwater can be obtained as Excel table from NRC EIONET Freshwater interest group library (subfolder Codelists of substances for water quality reporting):
-Groundwater: Nutrients + specification of Preferred SoE Nutrients, Potentially Low Values and Potentially High Values (outliers),
-Groundwater: Hazardous Substances + specification of Preferred SoE Haz.Subst., Potentially Low Values and Potentially High Values (outliers)
at http://forum.eionet.europa.eu/nrc-eionet-freshwater/library/wise-soe-reporting-2013/codelists-substances-water-quality-reporting
