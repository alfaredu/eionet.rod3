Pollutants into groundwater for which measures shall be established (Article 6): 
(a) hazardous substances, in particular those belonging to the families or groups of pollutants referred to in points 1 to 6 of Annex VIII to Directive 2000/60/EC, as well as in points 7 to 9 of that Annex, where these are considered to be hazardous;
(b) pollutants listed in Annex VIII to Directive 2000/60/EC which are not considered hazardous, and any other non-hazardous pollutants not listed in that Annex considered by Member States to present an existing or potential risk of pollution.
