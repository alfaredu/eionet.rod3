The main elements are defined in the Annex I to the Directive : delimitation of the District, designation of competent authority, and further detailed in the guidance. The 2004-2005 reporting sheets were agreed for the purpose of the electronic reporting in WISE linked to obligations under article 3 and article 5 of the WFD. The data were uploaded to the WISE-WFD prototype at JRC site and an XML scheme was provided. Reports have been moved to Central Data Repository (EEA Reportnet).
