Standing Committee Resolution No. 2 (1993) on the scope of Articles 8 and 9 of the Bern Convention resolved that national reports on exceptions made from the provisions of Articles 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 of the Convention shall cover only: 1) general exceptions; 2) individual exceptions if they are so numerous as to result in a generalised practice; 3) individual exceptions concerning more than ten individuals of a species; and 4) individual exceptions concerning individuals of endangered or vulnerable populations of species.
