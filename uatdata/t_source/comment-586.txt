Reports from the Commission to the Council and the European Parliament on the Statistics of the number of animals used for experimental and other scientific purposes in the Member States of the European Union can be found at http://ec.europa.eu/environment/chemicals/lab_animals/home_en.htm. The reports include forms to be used for the reporting. 
In addition to the reporting obligations included here, Member States shall furnish information to the Commission on their legislation and administrative practice that relates to animal experiments, including requirements to be satisfied prior to the marketing of products; they shall also supply factual information on experiments carried out in their territory and on authorizations or any other administrative particulars pertaining to these experiments.
