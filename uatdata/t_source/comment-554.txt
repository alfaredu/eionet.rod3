The ODS Regulation (EC) No 1005/2009, is a recast of Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000 and its amendments.
The following amendments apply to the ODS Regulation:
• Commission Decision on the use of controlled substances as process agents under Article 8(4) 
• Regulation on substances that deplete the ozone layer, with regard to the critical uses of halons  amending the ODS Regulation
