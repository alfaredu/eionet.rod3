The deadline for transposition of this Directive in Member States was 25/11/2009. 

Member States shall take appropriate steps to coordinate the application of this Directive and that of Directive 2000/60/EC focusing on opportunities for improving efficiency, information exchange and for achieving common synergies and benefits having regard to the environmental objectives laid down in Article 4 of Directive 2000/60/EC. 
There are operational links between the directives regarding implementation cycle, competent authorities (river basin districts), characterisation of river basin districts (flood maps), public participation, and programme of measures.  See for the Water Framework Directive (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/516).
