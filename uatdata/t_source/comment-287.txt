The Regulation has been applied 09/05/1994.

Commission Decision 94/721/EC of 21 October 1994 adapting pursuant to Article 42 (3), Annexes II, III and IV of Council Regulation 93/259/EEC on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31994D0721);
Commission Decision 96/660/EC of 14 November 1996 adapting pursuant to Article 42(3), Annex II to Council Regulation (EEC) 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of wastes within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31996D0660);
Council Regulation (EC) No 120/97 of 20 January 1997 amending Regulation (EC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31997R0120);
Commission Decision 98/368/EC of 18 May 1998 adapting, pursuant to Article 42(3) Annexes II and III to Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31998D0368);
Commission Regulation (EC) No 2408/98 of 6 November 1998 amending Annex V to Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31998R2408);
Commission Decision 1999/816/EC of 24 November 1999 adapting, pursuant to Articles 16(1) and 42(3), Annexes II, III, IV and V to Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community (CELEX No 31999D0816); 
Commission Regulation (EC) No 2557/2001 of 28 December 2001 amending Annex V of Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 on the supervision and control of shipments of waste within, into and out of the European Community(CELEX No 32001R2557).
The Regulation is supplemented by:
Commission Decision 1999/412/EC of 3 June 1999 concerning a questionnaire for the reporting obligation of Member States pursuant to Article 41(2) of Council Regulation (EEC) No 259/93 (CELEX No 31999D0412).
According to Article 41(1) of the Regulation Member States shall send to the Commission a copy of the yearly report to the Basel Convention (drawn up in accordance with Article 13 (3) of the Convention).
