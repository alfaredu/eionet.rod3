The first International Tropical Timber Agreement was negotiated in 1983 with a limited life span under the auspices of the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD).  The current ITTA was negotiated in 1994 and is the successor agreement to the ITTA (1983). The ITTA is being renegotiated during 2005/2006 under the auspices of the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD).
