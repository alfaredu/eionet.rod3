The purpose of this Directive is to approximate the laws of the Member States on the controlled elimination of PCBs. The Member States must take necessary measures concerning controlled disposal of PCBs, the decontamination or disposal of equipment containing PCBs and/or the disposal of used PCBs in order to eliminate them completely. 
Member States must make an inventory of large equipment containing PCBs, adopt a plan for disposal of inventoried equipment, and make outlines for collection and disposal of non inventoried equipment
