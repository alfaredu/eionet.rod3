According to Article 22 (1) the following will be repealed seven years after the date of entry into force of this Directive (22/12/2000): Directive 75/440/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/202), Decision 77/795/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/36), Directive 79/869/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/213).(2)The following shall be repealed 13 years after the date of entry into force of this Directive: Directive 78/659/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/210), Directive 79/923/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/214), Directive 80/68/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/217), Directive 76/464/EEC (http://rod.eionet.europa.eu/instruments/205).

The new Groundwater Directive (Directive 2006/118/EC) and Environmental Quality Standards Directive (Directive 2008/105/EC) are the daughter directives to this Directive. See under "Related instrument(s)".
