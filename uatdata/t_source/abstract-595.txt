The questionnaire set out in the Annex is used for annual reporting under the air quality framework directive and its daughter directives. (of OJ L156, 30 April 2004, p.78-125).
It repeals and replaces the previous questionnaire Council Decision 2001/839/EC per 29 April 2004.
The daughter directives set up under the Air quality framework directive can be found in ROD by following the link "Parent legislative instrument" on this page.
