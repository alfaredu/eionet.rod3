The Directive 88/347/EEC addresses the following substances: aldrin, dieldrin, endrin, isodrin, hexachlorobenzene, hexachlorobutadiene and chloroform; in line with developments in scientific knowledge relating principally to the toxicity, persistence and accumulation of the substances referred to in living organisms and sediments, or improvement in the best technical means available.
The Directive 90/415/EEC addresses the following substances: 1-2-dichloroethane, trichloroethylene, perchloroethylene and trichlorobenzene; in line with developments in scientific knowledge relating principally to the toxicity, persistence and accumulation of the substances referred to in living organisms and sediments, or improvement in the best technical means available.
