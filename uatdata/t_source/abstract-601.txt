"In 1982, at the third Conference in Copenhagen, Denmark a Joint Decleration was agreed upon by the three countries. According to the Joint Declaration the Wadden Sea countries declare their intention to coordinate their activities and measures to implement a number of international legal instruments in the field of natural environmental protection, amongst others the Ramsar Convention and the EC Bird Directive, for a comprehensive protection of the Wadden Sea region as a whole, including its flora and fauna.
