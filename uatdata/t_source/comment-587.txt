Besides the reporting obligations included here, Member States have a number of obligations for informing the Commission and other Member States as part of the procedures for evaluation and authorization of biocidal products. 
The first and second phases of the review programme referred to in Article 16(2) have been established in amendments to the directive: Commission Regulation (EC) No 1896/2000, Commission Regulation (EC) No 1687/2002, Commission Regulation (EC) No 2032/2003, Commission Regulation (EC) No 1048/2005 and Commission Regulation (EC) No 1849/2006.
